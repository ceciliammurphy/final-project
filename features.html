Trulytics Features

Trulytics is designed to present social media analytics in a clearer and more useful way. Rather than only showing surface-level numbers, the prototype focuses on patterns, comparisons, and context that help users better understand their content and audience.

Follower Growth

[Prototype Screenshot: Follower Growth]

The follower growth feature tracks how an account's audience changes over time. Instead of only showing a single follower count, this view helps users see whether growth is steady, stalled, or influenced by specific moments. By visualizing growth over time, Trulytics makes it easier to identify patterns and better understand what may be influencing visibility.

Engagement Trends

[Prototype Screenshot: Engagement Trends]

Engagement trends focus on how likes, comments, and other interactions shift across time. This feature helps users move beyond isolated post performance and instead look at broader patterns in how audiences respond. In a social media environment where users often adjust their posting habits based on feedback, this feature provides a clearer picture of what kind of content is consistently resonating and when engagement begins to change.

Content Performance

[Prototype Screenshot: Content Performance]

The content performance feature compares individual posts to show which ones generate the most response. This can help users identify stronger-performing content and notice patterns across captions, timing, or post type. Trulytics organizes these insights in a more transparent and readable way so users can make more informed decisions about what they share.

Audience Insights

[Prototype Screenshot: Audience Insights]

Audience insights focus on how people interact with content over time. Rather than only measuring popularity, this feature helps users think about behavior, habits, and response patterns within their audience. This matters because social media is not just about numbers; it is also about understanding relationships, visibility, and communication.

Why These Features Matter

Together, these features reflect the larger purpose of Trulytics: helping users understand social media performance in a way that feels more complete, transparent, and useful. Social media users often rely on metrics to interpret success, visibility, and audience response, but many existing tools present that information in limited or overwhelming ways. Trulytics reframes analytics as something that should support understanding rather than confusion.
