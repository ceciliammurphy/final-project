Welcome to Trulytics

Trulytics is a prototype analytics dashboard designed to help users better understand follower growth, posting activity, and post engagement patterns. The dashboard combines exported Instagram data with manually collected interaction metrics to show how clearer analytics design can make social media performance easier to interpret.

Prototype

Why Trulytics?

Most social media platforms provide metrics such as likes, follower counts, and post interactions, but those numbers often lack context. Users frequently adapt their posting behavior based on engagement patterns, yet the tools available to interpret those patterns can feel limited or incomplete. As social media becomes more integrated into personal, creative, and professional life, the demand for clearer analytics continues to grow.

Key Features

Follower Growth

Track how audience size changes over time using exported follower data.

Following Growth

Compare follower patterns with changes in accounts followed.

Post Interactions

Visualize manually collected interaction totals, including likes, comments, and sends.

Posting Activity

Use post timestamps to identify patterns in posting frequency over time.

Our Why

Engagement metrics influence how users interpret success, identity, and visibility online. Analytics shape posting decisions, content strategies, and even self-perception. Trulytics explores how clearer insights can help users better understand their digital presence by organizing follower data, posting behavior, and interaction metrics into a more readable dashboard.
